Description:

SmartCity Grid: IoT Infrastructure for Urban Efficiency

Description:

SmartCity Grid is an IT initiative focused on creating a connected urban infrastructure using the Internet of Things (IoT). This project involves deploying sensors and smart devices throughout the city to collect and analyze data for optimizing various services, such as traffic management, waste disposal, and energy consumption. SmartCity Grid aims to transform urban living by promoting efficiency, sustainability, and a higher quality of life.
